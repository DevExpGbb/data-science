# Prompt 1: Water Operations Briefing (Custom Agent)

## Troublesome scenario
Data scientists are asked for an executive-ready narrative, but raw records are noisy and operational teams need a concise decision brief in minutes.

## How Copilot helps
A custom Copilot agent can be tuned to behave like a water-operations analyst: summarize risk, spotlight hotspot sites, and translate technical results into action language.

## Sample prompt
"Using `../data/water_technicians_collections.csv`, create a short business brief that defines the water quality process, identifies risk hotspots by site, and suggests next-week mitigation priorities for field leadership."

## Agent customization
Use the companion `agent-customization.yml` to keep responses concise, operational, and stakeholder-focused.
